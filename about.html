"Hello, Musical World!"

BA Sounds
and its background.

Welcome to Music Teaching Online, a company conceived through the frustrations of not being able to find a local music teacher for our kids, or when we did find a teacher, who was often some distance away, lessons were missed due to traffic, delayed meetings or bad weather!

The founder of Music Teaching Online, who has over 25 years of experience as a music educator, initially set up an online help
group to provide students and parents just like us with support, but as demand significantly grew and technology advanced, we decided to share this help group to a wider audience and make it easier for you to access fantastic music teachers, not only locally but worldwide.
